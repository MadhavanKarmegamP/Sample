Our Story

Where the City Becomes Your View

Perched above the pulse of Guindy, Heights Rooftop Restaurant transforms an ordinary dinner into a memory. With the Chennai skyline stretching before you and the warm glow of city lights below, every evening here is a celebration of the night. Whether it's a quiet dinner for two or a lively gathering with friends — Heights is where moments happen.
